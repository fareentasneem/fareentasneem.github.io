About Me

CSE (Computer Science & Engineering) Graduate from University of Chittagong, Bangladesh. I have been venturing into research since 2020 in affiliation with the CSECU-DSG group. My research endeavors cover various themes in NLP such as opinion mining, information retrieval from social-media contents, and particularly intrigued by multi-modalities and crisis informatics.


My undergraduate thesis entitled An Early Fusion of Deep Neural Networks to Identify Multimodal Informative Disaster Tweets proposes a multimodal framework to address the challenges of identifying informative disaster tweets and further classifying them into humanitarian categories. [Poster]
